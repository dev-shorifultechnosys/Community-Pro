Our Mission & Vision

InUnity exists to transform communities through trauma-informed counseling, empowerment programs, and inclusive support systems. We believe in the inherent strength within every individual and community, waiting to be nurtured and realized.

Our vision is a world where every person, regardless of background, has access to healing resources, supportive communities, and opportunities for personal and collective growth.

OUR SERVICES

Comprehensive Support for Your Journey

We offer a holistic range of trauma-informed services designed to meet you where you are and support your unique path to healing and empowerment.

Trauma-Informed Care Excellence

Compassionate, evidence-based approaches tailored to your unique journey

Individual Counseling

One-on-one trauma-informed therapy sessions tailored to your specific needs, focusing on healing, growth, and personal transformation.

  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Evidence-based approaches
  • Flexible scheduling options

Group Therapy

Facilitated group sessions that foster connection, shared healing, and collective growth in a safe, supportive environment.

  • Peer support networks
  • Specialized topic groups
  • Cultural affinity spaces

Family Counseling

Strengthen family bonds and improve communication through culturally sensitive family therapy and conflict resolution.

  • Intergenerational healing
  • Communication skills building
  • Culturally informed approaches

Trauma Recovery

Specialized trauma-informed care using evidence-based modalities to support healing from complex trauma and PTSD.

  • EMDR therapy available
  • Somatic experiencing
  • Safety-focused approach

Youth Empowerment

Age-appropriate counseling and mentorship programs designed to help young people navigate challenges and build resilience.

  • Identity development support
  • Leadership training
  • Peer mentorship programs

Workshops & Training

Educational workshops, community training sessions, and professional development opportunities on trauma-informed practices.

  • Community education
  • Professional development
  • Custom organizational training

COMMUNITY PROGRAMS

Growing Together Through Shared Experiences

Our community programs create spaces for connection, learning, and collective healing, fostering a sense of belonging for all participants.

Women's Empowerment Circle

Weekly gatherings for women

A safe space for women to share experiences, build connections, and support each other's journeys of healing and empowerment. Topics include self-care, boundary setting, trauma recovery, and personal growth.

Every Tuesday 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM Free

Youth Leadership Academy

Ages 14-24 program

Empowering young leaders through skill-building workshops, mentorship, and community service projects. Participants develop leadership skills, cultural pride, and tools for navigating challenges.

Every Saturday 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM $25/month

Healing Through Stories

Monthly storytelling circles where community members share narratives of resilience, healing, and transformation.

Art Therapy Workshop

Wednesdays | 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Mindfulness & Wellness

Cultural Heritage Celebration

Monthly events celebrating diverse cultural traditions, foods, music, and stories. These gatherings honor the rich tapestry of our community's heritage while building cross-cultural understanding and appreciation.

  • Traditional music and dance performances
  • Cultural food tastings and cooking demos
  • Storytelling from elders and community members
  • Traditional crafts and art workshops
